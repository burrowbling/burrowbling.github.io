WHO IS SAM TOEMAN?

Samantha Toeman is an artist-gamedeveloper-writer from New York studying at the Rhode Island School of Design. She grew up in San Francisco on a big hill with five chickens.

She likes when her fingers get cold in January and she has trouble typing, having stacks of magazines in the bathroom, grapefruits, datasets, rain, ballpoint pens, email, good honey, and compound words.

This winter, she's passing the time making candles, drinking tea, listening to the radio, and sitting on the floor.

If you'd like to support her and her artwork, insert a few quarters into her Patreon. You'll get a lovely newsletter, free drawings mailed to you, and some demos of her unreleased games.
