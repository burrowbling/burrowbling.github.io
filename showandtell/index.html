what do i bring?
You should bring something of importance to you, that you really like.

Try not to bring something you made.

You can bring an artwork, image, short video or audio piece, or poem. Or, you can bring a physical object, like a rock, bowl, or sculpture. If you want, you can even show a dance or another cool trick/skill.
